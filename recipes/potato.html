Creamy Leek and Potato Soup

There is nothing better on a cold or rainy day than a nice hot bowl of soup, and there is no soup more filling than creamy leak and potato soup. This soup isn't your traditional french vichyssoie, which is a blended soup the color of cream. This potato leak soup will be a little more rustic, but much more delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Potatos
  • 4 Large Leeks
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter
  • 7 Cups Chicken or Vegetable Broth
  • Chives for Garnish
  • 3 Sprigs of Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Melt the butter over medium heat in a large soup pot
  2. Add the leeks and garlic and cook, stirring regularly, until soft and wilted, about 10 minutes
  3. Add the potatoes, broth, thyme, salt and pepper to pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and turn the heat down to low. Simmer for 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are very soft.
  5. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve into bowls, garnish with the thyme and chives, and enjoy!
