Baked Atlantic Salmon

There are few cuts of meat healthier, easier to cook, or more delicious than a salmon fillet, and i don't know of any that are all three! Topped with dill and a with sides of lemon wedges, this will make a perfect weeknight dinner meal! This recipe shows you how to bake a very simple and delicious peace of a salmon to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filet
  • 2 Tbsp of Butter
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Dill for Garnish
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Put salmon on baking sheet and cover with butter and minced garlic
  2. Bake at 400F for 15 minutes, or until fish flakes apart
  3. Pull out of oven and garnish with dill
  4. Serve with wedges of lemon
